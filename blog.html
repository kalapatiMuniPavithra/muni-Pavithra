About Sri Kalahasthi

Sri Kalahasthi, located in Andhra Pradesh, is renowned for the ancient Sri Kalahasteeswara Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. Known as one of the Pancha Bhoota Sthalams, it represents the element of air. Highlights of Sri Kalahasthi

  • Sri Kalahasteeswara Temple: A 1000-year-old temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Vayu Linga: One of the unique Shiva Lingas representing the air element.
  • Architecture: Dravidian-style architecture with intricate carvings.
  • Religious Significance: Believed to be a sacred site for Rahu-Ketu pooja.
  • Nearby Attractions: Tirupati, a short distance away, makes it a perfect pilgrimage destination.
